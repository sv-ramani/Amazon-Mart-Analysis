# Amazon-Mart-Analysis
📊 Amazon Sales Dashboard – Power BI

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Amazon sales performance, providing a 360° view of sales, profitability, and order trends across categories, regions, and time.

🔍 Overview

This dashboard transforms raw Amazon sales data into actionable business insights, helping stakeholders quickly identify top-performing categories, regional trends, and profitability patterns.

✨ Key Features


KPI Cards – Total Sales, Total Profit, and Total Count of Orders at a glance
Sales by Category & Sub-Category – Column and bar charts to identify top and underperforming product lines
Sales by Region – Pie chart showing regional sales distribution
Sales by Month – Line chart to track sales trends over time
Profit vs. Sales by Month – Combo chart comparing profit and sales trends side by side
Interactive Slicers – Filter the entire report by Region and Country
Decomposition Tree – Drill down into Sum of Sales across Country, City, Segment, Category, and Sub-Category for root-cause analysis


🛠️ Tech Stack


Power BI Desktop – Report design and visualization
Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
DAX – Calculated measures (Sales, Profit, Order Count)


📁 Dataset

The dashboard is built on an Amazon sales dataset (Amazon Mart), containing fields such as:


Order ID, Order Date
Category, Sub-Category
Region, Country, City, Segment
Sales, Profit


📌 Insights You Can Explore


Which categories and sub-categories drive the most sales?
Which regions are the most profitable?
How do sales and profit trends move together month over month?
Where are the biggest opportunities for margin improvement?
